Participation as a speaker

Should you be interested to participate at TIIF 2023 as a speaker, please contact the Programme Team of TIIF indicating your affiliation and position, contact details as well as preferred sessions (maximum 3 sessions in order of preference). Please also include your short bio. The Programme Team will contact you about the opportunities to participate as a speaker as soon as possible. We thank you for your interest in TIIF and look forward to welcoming you in Tashkent.
