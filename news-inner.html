Innovations in the field of mechanical engineering were discussed at the international investment forum in Tashkent

Citizens of 86 countries can visit Uzbekistan without a visa. A valid passport is required for entry into Uzbekistan.

Citizens of Azerbaijan, Armenia, Belarus, Georgia, Kazakhstan, Moldova, Russia, and Ukraine can visit Uzbekistan without a visa. Citizens of the Kyrgyz Republic are permitted to stay for up to 60 days. For citizens of China, including the Hong Kong special administrative region, a visa-free regime has been established for entry into Uzbekistan for a period of no more than 7 days through the country’s international airports.
