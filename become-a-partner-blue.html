Opportunities (preferences) for partners of the Tashkent International Investment Forum

Sponsor's opportunity to participate in the event

Placement of sponsor's logo on the official website of the event indicating the status of sponsor with an active link to the company's website during the preparation and holding of the event

Publication of advertising posts on the official pages of the event in the social networks (Facebook and Instagram) about a sponsor of the event, indicating the status of sponsor

Placement of sponsor's logo on the starting banner before broadcasting events on the YouTube platform live on the official TIIF channel

Placement of sponsor's logo on the press wall of the event (Press wall is installed at the location of the event) indicating the status of sponsor

Verbal announcement from the announcer/moderator of the event about a partner before the start of official events (During gathering of guests)

Displaying sponsor's logo with indicating the status in the background image of the event on LED screens (During gathering of guests)

Opportunity to take part in formation of handouts for the participants of the event

Opportunity to hold own special events within the framework of the Forum (presentations, promotions, business breakfasts, etc.).

Provision of a venue for a press conference with the possibility of an exclusive interview for interested mass media (information about the date of press conference is provided for the press additionally)

Assistance in organizing bilateral meetings and providing rooms for negotiations within the framework of the Forum
